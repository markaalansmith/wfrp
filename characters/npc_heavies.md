# Squad: The Heavies (Middenheim Watch)
**Status:** Active | **Unit:** Surface Tactical / Heavy Support  
**Leader:** Kroll (Lead Veteran)  
**Composition:** Kroll, "Iron-Side" Gunter, "Lefty" Hans

---

## I. SQUAD OVERVIEW
**The Unit:** Known as "The Surface Anvil." While Gotthard’s tunnel specialists move through the muck, the Heavies represent the cold, polished weight of Vogler’s authority. They are career soldiers, not gutter-skulkers; they are the ones you trust to hold a line when the world ends behind them.

**Operational Doctrine:** They provide the heavy iron needed to anchor a position. They transition between civil crowd control and lethal military engagement with the practiced ease of men who have survived a dozen border skirmishes.

**Appearance:** Professional Middenheim Watch uniforms. Striking blue tabards worn over polished mail and breastplates. They don't skitter or hide; they look like the Law.

---

## II. THE PERSONNEL (Middenheim Watch Veterans)

### **Kroll (The Lead Veteran)**
* **Role:** Squad Leader / Tactical Anchor.
* **Description:** A career soldier who treats a halberd like an extension of his own arm. He is the vital bridge between the Sergeant’s "gutter-logic" and the Watch’s rigid "regulations."
* **Audit:** Cool-headed under pressure. He is the squad's moral and tactical center.

### **"Iron-Side" Gunter (The Oak)**
* **Role:** Heavy Vanguard / Breaker.
* **Description:** A stout man who looks like he was carved out of an ancient oak tree. He doesn't say much—mostly because he hasn't blinked in three years—but his survival in border skirmishes speaks for him.
* **Audit:** Carries a heavy mace and shield. He is the physical wall of the squad.

### **"Lefty" Hans (The Eyes)**
* **Role:** Marksman / Lookout.
* **Description:** A veteran spearman with a scarred face and a missing pinky finger. He serves as the squad's eyes; nothing moves in a crowd or a corridor that Hans doesn't spot.
* **Audit:** Specialist with the heavy spear and crossbow.
