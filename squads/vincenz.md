# NPC: VINCENZ (THE TEMPLAR)
**Career:** Witch Hunter | **Race:** Human  
**Status:** Active | **Role:** Investigator & Holy Executioner

---

## I. BIOGRAPHY & PROFILE
Vincenz is a man of "Grim Sincerity." He doesn't seek the spotlight; he seeks the rot. He has spent years performing a "Clinical Scrutiny" of the Empire's shadows, treating heresy like a festering wound that requires a hot iron. He views Gotthard as a "Foundational Necessity"—a man of the dirt who can see what the high-born priests ignore through their silk veils.

* **Marksmanship:** *Field Note:* A +10% to all BS tests. He treats a crossbow bolt as a final punctuation mark.
* **Disarm:** *Field Note:* Reallocating an opponent's "Hardware" to the mud.
* **Intimidate:** *Field Note:* A "Forceful Intervention" of the spirit. His stare tastes like a cold wind from the Aethyr.
* **Public Speaking:** *Field Note:* Turning a crowd into a lynch-mob before they’ve finished their lunch break.
* **Magic Resistance:** *Field Note:* A +20% to WP tests to resist the corruptive "wind" of the Great Enemy.
* **Strike to Injure:** *Field Note:* Finding the anatomical gap to deliver a rather rude invitation for humors to exit the body.

## IV. TACTICAL NOTE
Vincenz is the "Hammer" to Gotthard's "Chisel." He provides the moral and legal weight to the mission, ensuring that once the evidence is found, the "Erasure" of the enemy is final and absolute. In combat, he is a "Ballad-Ender," striking with clinical precision while others are still negotiating with the mud.
