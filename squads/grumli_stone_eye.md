# NPC: Grumli Stone-Eye
**Career:** Master Engineer | **Race:** Dwarf  
**Status:** Active | **Role:** Sapper & Structural Specialist

---

## I. BIOGRAPHY & PROFILE
Grumli is a veteran Engineer—a man whose career is split evenly between being a scientist and a siege-breaker. He doesn't see a wall as a barrier; he sees it as a mathematical problem involving structural tension and the correct application of black powder. He is Ulrican-leaning in his faith, preferring the cold reality of the "City-Rock" to the finery of the South.

## II. SKILLS & ABILITIES

* **Stone-Sense (Dwarf Racial):** Can detect sloping passages, new construction, and shifting masonry on a successful $Int$ test.
* **Engineer:** Mastery in identifying and repairing mechanical devices, traps, and structural weaknesses.
* **Mining:** Expert at tunneling and identifying "dead-falls" or load-bearing pillars.
* **Identify Magic Sign (Engineering):** Specialist ability to spot where magic has been used to reinforce or manipulate stone.
* **Combat Engineering:** $+10\%$ to all tests involving the use of explosives or siege engines.
* **Hatred (Skaven/Goblins):** It's a Dwarf thing. $+10\%$ to WS when fighting rat-men or greenskins.
* **Secret Language (Guild Tongue - Engineering):** Technical jargon used for high-level engineering talk that confuses "Dull-Eyes" (non-Dwarfs).
