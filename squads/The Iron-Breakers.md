# Squad: The Iron-Breakers (Dwarf Reinforcements)
**Status:** Active | **Unit:** Specialist Heavy Support / Siege-Breakers  
**Composition:** Bori "The Breaker", Thrain "Soot-Beard"

---

## I. THE PERSONNEL (Dwarf Specialists)

### **Bori "The Breaker"**
* **Role:** Front-line Tank / Heavy Vanguard.
* **Description:** A dwarf who looks like a walking boulder. He doesn't just hold a line; he becomes the line. He carries a shield the size of a tavern door and wears enough mail to sink a rowboat.
* **Audit:** Bori is the squad's "Mobile Barricade." He is the man you stand behind when the black powder starts cooking or the arrows start flying.

### **Thrain "Soot-Beard"**
* **Role:** Expert Sapper / Infiltrator.
* **Description:** Covered in a permanent layer of black-powder residue and stone dust. He carries a staggering array of specialized chisels, probes, and hammers—enough to dismantle a fortress from the inside out.
* **Audit:** Thrain doesn't believe in "impassable" walls. He views every structure as a puzzle that can be solved with a well-placed wedge or a precisely struck chisel.

## III. SKILLS & ABILITIES

### **Bori's Specialties**
* **Shield Wall:** Bori can interpose his massive shield to provide $+2$ AP to an adjacent ally against ranged attacks.
* **Immovable Object:** Bori gains $+20\%$ to any test to resist being knocked down or pushed back.
* **Strike to Stun:** Expert at using his hammer to leave enemies reeling rather than just dead.

### **Thrain's Specialties**
* **Fortress Breaker:** $+20\%$ to any test involving the sabotage of masonry, gates, or mechanical locks.
* **Stone-Sense:** (Dwarf Racial) Detect sloping passages and shifting masonry.
* **Fine-Work:** Thrain can use his sapper’s tools as a Master-work Lockpick kit.

## V. TACTICAL NOTE
These two are the "Brute Force" and the "Finesse" of the Dwarfen contingent. **Bori** ensures the squad lives long enough to reach the target, and **Thrain** ensures that once they arrive, no door or wall stays standing. Use them to anchor a defensive position or to breach a fortified "Ozone" leak.
