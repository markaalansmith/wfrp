# Squad: The Tunnel Guards (Middenheim Watch)
**Status:** Active | **Unit:** Special Sub-Surface Tactical Division  
**Leader:** Sergeant Gotthard  
**Composition:** Hog-Eye, Stevo, Janko

---

## I. SQUAD OVERVIEW
**The Unit:** Unlike Gotthard’s origins in the gutter, these men are **Professional Watchmen** of a specialized tactical unit. They are the Middenheim Watch’s answer to the "Under-City" problem—equipped for the damp, the dark, and the desperate. They move with the discipline of soldiers and hit with the crushing force of falling masonry.

**Operational Doctrine:** They are trained for "Dirty Realism" combat—breaching barricades, clearing tunnels, and maintaining structural integrity under fire. They are the "Sewer-Shields" of the White Wolf, treated with a mix of awe and pity by the surface-level recruits.

**Appearance:** Dark, decent wool and canvas uniforms—deep charcoal greys and forest greens. No glint or shine to betray their position in the dark. They carry the heavy scent of waterproof boot-oil, stale tobacco, and old blood.

---

## II. THE PERSONNEL (Middenheim Watch Specialists)

### **Hog-Eye (The Heavy Hitter)**
* **Role:** Front-line Breacher / Heavy Infantry.
* **Description:** A mountain of a man with a meat-grinder of a fist. He treats his Tunnel Axe like a surgeon treats a scalpel—if the surgeon were ten feet tall and made of muscle.
* **Current Task:** Acting as the "Watch Anvil" guarding the "Golden Hand" in the cells.
* **Audit:** Slow to speak, but the first to charge when the Sergeant gives the word.

### **Stevo (The Tinkerer)**
* **Role:** Structural Specialist / Trap-Breaker.
* **Description:** A cynical veteran with a "Watchman’s Nose" for secret doors. He understands the "manual" logic of traps and masonry better than any University scholar.
* **Audit:** A steady hand with a Light Crossbow; he knows exactly when to keep his head down and when to pull the trigger.

### **Janko (The Runner)**
* **Role:** Tactical Scout / Messenger.
* **Description:** The youngest and fastest of the division. While twitchy from the horrors of the deep, his aim with a crossbow is true even when the ozone starts crackling and the shadows start biting.
* **Audit:** High Agility and Initiative; the squad's primary eyes in the lightless void.
