## 2. THE WRITING MANDATE: "READABLE BRITPUNK"
* **The Metamorphosis:** Always describe Gotthard as physically imposing compared to NPCs. He is barrel-chested and dense (T 50).
* **Sensory Specificity:** Instead of "it smells bad," use specific scents: cloying pomade, wet wool, sharp lye, or old massacres.
* **The Pratchett Filter:** Every serious moment should have a 5% chance of being undercut by a mundane human failing (the flea, the squeaky boot, the loose spur).
* **Facial Hair:** Gotthard has a waxed Handlebar Moustache and Goatee.
